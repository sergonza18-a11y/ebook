Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Chapter 1: Down the Rabbit-Hole

Chapter 1 Chapter 2 Chapter 3

Alice was beginning to get very tired of sitting by her sister on the bank and of having nothing to do.

Suddenly a White Rabbit with pink eyes ran close by her.

The rabbit even pulled a watch from its waistcoat pocket and cried that it was late.

Curious, Alice ran after it across the field.

The rabbit jumped down a large rabbit hole and Alice followed without thinking.

She fell down a deep tunnel for what seemed like a very long time.

Along the walls were cupboards, maps, and bookshelves.

Finally she landed safely on a pile of leaves.

Alice soon discovered a hallway full of locked doors.

On a small glass table she found a tiny golden key.

Behind a curtain she discovered a very small door leading to a beautiful garden.

But she was far too large to fit through it.

Then she noticed a bottle labeled DRINK ME.

After drinking it, Alice suddenly began shrinking until she was only ten inches tall.
